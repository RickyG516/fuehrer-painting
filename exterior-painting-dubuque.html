Our Exterior Process

Four Steps to a Lasting Exterior

No shortcuts at any stage. This is how we get paint jobs that look good in year one and year seven.

1
Power Wash & Prep
We power wash the entire exterior, scrape any loose or failing paint, sand rough spots, and inspect every surface for damage that needs addressing before paint goes on.
2
Caulk & Prime
Every gap, joint, and crack gets caulked to stop moisture infiltration. Bare wood and repaired areas get primed with high-quality exterior primer for full adhesion.
3
Two-Coat Application
Premium paint, two coats, applied in the right conditions. We never rush drying time. Each coat gets full coverage before the next one goes on.
4
Detail & Clean
Trim touched up, drop cloths picked up, overspray checked, landscaping cleared. We walk the whole exterior with you before we consider the job done.
