How Commercial Jobs Work

5-Step Commercial Process

Every commercial project goes through a documented process so nothing falls through the cracks.

1
Site Walk & Bid
We walk the property, document the scope, and deliver a written bid with full line-item breakdown. No ballpark numbers.
2
Schedule Around You
We build the work schedule around your business hours. After-hours, weekends, phased by zone — whatever minimizes disruption to your operations.
3
Surface Prep
All surfaces prepped — cleaned, patched, sanded, and primed where needed. Proper prep is what separates a commercial-grade finish from a quick coat.
4
Application
Right product for the environment. Spray, brush, or roll depending on the surface and setting. Two coats minimum. Consistent finish throughout.
5
Quality Sign-Off
Full walkthrough with facility manager or owner before we sign off. Any punch list items addressed immediately. You don't approve until it's right.
