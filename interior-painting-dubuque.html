How It Works

Our 4-Step Interior Process

No surprises. No guesswork. Here's exactly what happens from your first call to the final walkthrough.

1
Color Consultation
We walk through your space together. You share what you're envisioning, we share what we know works. Paint samples go up, decisions get made with confidence.
2
Thorough Prep
Furniture moved and covered. Floors protected. Trim and fixtures masked. Holes patched, surfaces sanded, and everything primed where needed. The prep is what makes the finish.
3
Precision Painting
Premium paints, expert brush and roller work. We cut clean lines by hand and roll consistent texture wall to wall. Two coats minimum. No thin spots, no lap marks.
4
Final Walkthrough
We walk every painted surface with you before we pack up. Touch-ups addressed on the spot. You don't sign off until you're fully satisfied. That's the guarantee.
