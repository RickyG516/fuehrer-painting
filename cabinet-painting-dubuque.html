The Fuehrer Process

5 Steps to a Kitchen That Looks Brand New

Every cabinet job follows this exact sequence. No shortcuts. No exceptions.

1
Remove Doors & Hardware
All cabinet doors, drawer fronts, and hardware removed and labeled. Nothing gets painted in place that should be painted off-site.
2
Sand & De-grease
Every surface sanded to scuff and de-glossed. Thorough de-greasing removes kitchen residue that would cause adhesion failure.
3
Spray Prime
Bonding primer sprayed across all surfaces — boxes, doors, and drawer fronts. This is what makes the topcoat stick and last.
4
Spray Topcoat
Two coats of professional cabinet enamel, spray-applied. Smooth, even, factory-quality finish on every surface. No brush strokes. No roller texture.
5
Reinstall & Inspect
Hardware reinstalled, doors hung and adjusted. Full walkthrough together before we pack up — every door opens smooth, every finish is flawless.
