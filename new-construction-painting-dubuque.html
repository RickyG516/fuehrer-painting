New Construction Process

5 Phases. Zero Surprises.

New construction painting has a specific sequence. We know it, we follow it, and we communicate every step with your GC.

1
Pre-Drywall Walk
Walk the project with the GC. Document the paint spec, color selections, and timeline. No assumptions — everything confirmed in writing.
2
Primer Coat
Full primer coat on all drywall after tape and texture. We hit this phase on schedule so finish work can proceed without waiting on us.
3
Finish Coat After Trim
Finish walls and ceilings after trim is installed. Crisp cut lines on all casings, base, and crown. Floors and cabinets protected throughout.
4
Touch-Up After Punch List
We come back after final inspection and address every paint-related punch list item. Same crew, same paint, clean result. No excuses.
5
Final Approval
GC and owner walkthrough. We're on-site for the final sign-off and any last touch-ups get handled before we pack up for good.
