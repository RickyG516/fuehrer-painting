Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who We Are

Fuehrer Painting LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a licensed painting contractor based in Dubuque, Iowa, serving the Tri-State area including Dubuque, Dyersville, Galena, and Platteville.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to give us — when you fill out our estimate request form, call or email us directly, or submit a job application through our Careers page. This may include your name, phone number, email address, project address, and project details.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule appointments, coordinate your project, follow up on your satisfaction, or review job applications. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics tools to understand site usage in aggregate. This data is anonymous. We do not use retargeting ads, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Netlify for hosting and form processing, Google Fonts for typography, and Google Maps for location data. Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain contact and project information only as long as necessary to complete your project and meet legal requirements. Job application data is deleted after 60 days unless we move forward with employment.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold. Contact us below and we will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
