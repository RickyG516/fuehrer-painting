The Story

Meet Mark Fuehrer

Mark Fuehrer launched Fuehrer Painting LLC in 2020 with a simple belief: if you do the work right and treat people honestly, the business takes care of itself.

What he brought to that first job wasn't a brand new business — it was over a decade of hands-on painting experience. Mark learned the trade the right way: no shortcuts, no rushing, no compromising on prep work. In a market full of painters who roll and go, Mark built his reputation by being the guy who stayed until it was right.

That track record speaks for itself. Most of Fuehrer Painting's work comes from referrals — homeowners who loved their results telling neighbors, family, and friends. It's the product of doing things the way Mark believes they should be done.

Fuehrer Painting LLC is fully licensed and insured in the State of Iowa. Every job — from a single bedroom to a full commercial build — gets the same standard of care. That's not a promise. It's a track record.
