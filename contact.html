Common Questions

Before You Call — Quick Answers

How do I get a free estimate? ⌄
Fill out the form above or call us at (563) 213-4418. We'll schedule a time to see the project in person, measure what needs to be done, and give you a written estimate with a clear price — no vague ranges. Most estimates are delivered within 24-48 hours of the site visit.
How long does a typical interior painting project take? ⌄
A single room typically takes 1-2 days when you account for proper prep, drying time, and two coats. A full home interior can range from 3-7 days depending on square footage, ceiling height, and the number of doors and trim pieces involved. We give you a specific timeline in the estimate so you know exactly what to expect.
Do you move furniture and protect my floors? ⌄
Yes — always. We move and cover furniture, lay drop cloths on floors, and mask trim, fixtures, and anything that isn't being painted. We protect your home like it's our own. When we leave, we clean up completely. The only thing different about your space when we're done is that the paint looks great.
What brands of paint do you use? ⌄
We use professional-grade paints from Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore — the same brands used by the best painting contractors in the industry. We don't use builder-grade or budget paint products. If you have a preference, we can accommodate it. Premium materials are part of why our work lasts.
Are you licensed and insured? ⌄
Yes. Fuehrer Painting LLC is fully licensed in the State of Iowa and carries general liability and workers' compensation insurance. We can provide certificates of insurance for commercial clients, general contractors, and property managers on request. You're covered. Your property is covered. No exceptions.
